Spaghetti Carbonara - A Classic Italian Pasta Dish

Spaghetti Carbonara is a rich and creamy Italian pasta dish made with eggs, pancetta, Pecorino Romano cheese, and black pepper. Unlike many creamy pasta dishes, Carbonara achieves its luxurious texture without the use of heavy cream‚Äîonly using the silkiness of eggs and cheese.

This dish is a Roman classic, known for its bold flavors and simplicity. Perfect for a quick yet indulgent meal, Spaghetti Carbonara pairs beautifully with a crisp white wine and fresh bread.

Buon appetito!

Ingredients:

Ingredients for Spaghetti Carbonara (Serves 2-4)

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese, finely grated
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, finely grated
  • 1 clove garlic, whole (optional, for flavoring the pancetta)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

    • The steps to making a classic Spaghetti Carbonara

    Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • In a small bowl, whisk together the eggs and both cheeses until well combined.
    • Dice the pancetta or guanciale into small pieces.

    Step 2: Cook the Pasta & Pancetta

    • Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
    • Meanwhile, heat a pan over medium heat and add the diced pancetta (and garlic if using).
    • Cook until the pancetta is crispy and the fat has rendered (about 5 minutes).
    • Remove the garlic clove if used, then turn off the heat.

    Step 3: Combine the Sauce

    • Reserve about 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining the spaghetti.
    • Add the hot pasta directly into the pan with pancetta, tossing to coat in the rendered fat.
    • Remove the pan from heat and quickly stir in the egg and cheese mixture.
    • Use a little reserved pasta water, if needed, to create a silky sauce.
    • Season generously with black pepper and mix well.

    Step 4: Serve & Enjoy

    • Plate the spaghetti and sprinkle extra Pecorino Romano cheese on top.
    • Serve immediately with fresh black pepper.
    • Pair with a crisp white wine and fresh bread for the ultimate experience.
